About PawPal

We connect veterinarians and pet owners in a digital space to make pet care accessible, trustworthy, and modern. Our mission is to empower vets and deliver quality care from anywhere.

Who We Are

PawPal was founded with the vision to bring pet care into the digital era. We believe in seamless connection between veterinarians and pet parents — as trust, care, and ease matter the most.

Our platform supports registration, scheduling, video appointments, medical records, and more — enabling vets to manage their virtual practice effectively.

What We Do

At PawPal, we provide a platform that allows:

  • Veterinarians to register, manage their profile, and schedule online appointments.
  • Pet owners to browse, connect, and consult with vets.
  • Secure communication, medical record keeping, and seamless workflows.
